About PetOver

PetOver is a marketplace for pet lovers made by pet lovers. Never again do you have to worry about where to find a pet sitter before your next vacation, nor do you have to feel bad for asking your best friend for the hundredth time to watch your little companion while you're away.

Have you ever wondered what it would be like to have a pet but aren't ready to make that big step? This is a perfect opportunity to get to know different kinds of animals and feel the connection that so many people have with their pets. You even get to set your own rates!

It's not just about pet sitting though; you can even advertise your other pet-related services here! Are you a dog barber? Do you give kitty manicures? Are you a veterinarian with a private clinic? Here you can connect with pet owners in your area and reach out to people who would otherwise miss you.

PetOver was born out of necessity; our cats Freja and Loke need love and care while we're away. After having had every friend in our phone book take care of them a few times, it got harder and harder to keep asking for favors. We decided that we would create a social network where all pet lovers could gather around the common goal of taking care of our best friends of the animal kingdom.
